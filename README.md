# Digital Payment in India: Trends & Insights Dashboard
Power BI analysis of India's digital payment trends (2020-2024) with DAX-driven growth and market share insights

## Key Insights
- **UPI dominance:** Grew 74% annually (CAGR) and holds 45.6% of digital payment market share
- **Digital surge:** Total digital payments grew 215% from 2020 to 2024
- **Market leader:** PhonePe dominates the UPI app ecosystem
- **Channel shift:** Mobile payments significantly outpace internet banking
- **Traditional growth:** RTGS shows highest CAGR among legacy channels

## Technical Skills
- **DAX Measures:** YoY growth, CAGR, market share calculations
- **Functions:** CALCULATE, DIVIDE, DATEADD, filter context management
- **Visualizations:** Multi-page dashboard with trend analysis, comparative charts, quarterly patterns
- **Data Modeling:** Time intelligence, relationship optimization

## What I Learned
- Building complex DAX measures from scratch and managing filter context
- Designing two-page data narratives: "Overview" + "Comparative Analysis"
- Translating raw transaction volumes into business-ready KPIs
- Identifying actionable patterns in India's fintech evolution


## Data Source
RBI (Reserve Bank of India) and NPCI digital payment statistics (2020-2024)
